# nba-regression-analysis

For my SUPA (Syracuse University Project Advance) Statistics class final project, we were expected to use what we had learned to do research on real statistical issues. I chose to focus on Basketball from the perspective of a player hoping to help their team win by improving specific aspects of their game. Using historical data, I wanted to determine the areas of the game that would have the highest rate of return if a player focused on improving in that category. I took a look at NBA statistics that are commonly tracked over all games and decided to measure their impact on the game by finding their relationship with point differential. In other words, I wanted to see how much being slightly better in a particular skill impacts the amount you win a game by.
